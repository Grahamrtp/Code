What is bulldog coin?

Bulldog coin is a digital currency that rewards students for good behavior. Teachers will be given currency that they will hand out to student if they have done a good deed. Students willl be able to trade the currency among themselves and will also be able to redeem it for rewards.


Why use bulldog coin?

  1. Less polution
  2. kids learn about crypto
  3. kids learn finacial literacy
  4. kids get rewarded for good behavior
